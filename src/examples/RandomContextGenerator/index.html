The Random Context Generator App

Here is a solution to a problem of creating contexts for architectural models.

You can create a context by defining the site area and drawing a major road of the site. Upload a model to directly visualise in the context generated by you.

Move the point to move your design across the site and visualise in the browser.

(All dimensions in m.)
